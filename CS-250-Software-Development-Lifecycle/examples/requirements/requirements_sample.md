# Sample Requirements (CS-250)

## Functional
1. System shall allow users to log in with email and password.
2. System shall lock accounts after 5 failed login attempts.
3. Admins shall generate PDF reports of all users.

## Non-Functional
1. System shall handle 500 concurrent logins.  
2. System shall respond to dashboard queries in < 2 seconds.  
3. System shall store passwords using salted SHA-256 hashing.
